The Project

Rover Works addresses the gap in the market for affordable warehouse automation. [cite_start]While existing solutions often cost upwards of $30,000, our Modular Automated Guided Vehicle (MAGV) targets a price point near $2,000, making automation accessible for small facilities[cite: 51, 590].

[cite_start]The base unit is a robust 12V system capable of carrying 30 lbs at speeds up to 4 MPH[cite: 52]. [cite_start]It features a unique interchangeable top-module system that allows operators to swap between a Conveyor Module and a Pin Docking Unit in under 60 seconds[cite: 52, 247].

[cite_start]Navigation is handled via a sensor array and a custom-built AGV Planner Interface, allowing for precise line following, obstacle detection, and logic-based routing[cite: 96, 254].
